Finding

I’ve searched,
I’ve rambled,
I’ve conversed.
So what have I found?

I’ve ignored my skin,
Or stared at it like a stranger.
But now I'm starting to find myself,
Skin and all.
It was with me before
I became uncomfortable with it.

Searching, rambling, conversing.
This is not a comprehensive list.
I will forever continue to come to terms with,
and learn more about my skin.

But this is my one finding.
After years of ignoring it,
I see it.
My skin doesn’t sit idly,
Muffled by my efforts.
It speaks, and I speak with it now.
We have a lot to catch up on.
