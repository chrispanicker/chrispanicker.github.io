WHO IS JAI PAUL?


Jai Paul is a bit of an enigma in the music industry. I found out about him from a friend, who heard of him from a friend, who heard of him through a SoundCloud leak. His 2013 Album, "Leak 04-13(Bait Ones)" gave him critical acclaim, though the album itself is an unofficial release, separate from his label at the time, XL Records.
